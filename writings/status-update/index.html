Status Update

Today I begin work as the web designer at Co-op@Home. It’s sort of a “Part II” to Josiah’s “Thus Begin a New Era” as I am filling the role that he left.

Red River classes are over for me, so I’m done college classes for the foreseeable future. College has felt like both a long time and a short time, and it’s strange to be done. I made friends with a score of great young designers, and I’ll miss them along with their city, but I’ll try to visit. When I started college, I began every day by listening to The Weakerthans’ One Great City for the first couple of weeks. However, I don’t know if I hate Winnipeg anymore. I think I’ve grown rather fond of it.

But it’s good to be back. Good to be amongst friends. Good to be starting an exciting new job.

-JD
