Gig Poster

  • Graphic Design

@ Red River College

October 2013

One of our Graphic Design projects was to create a gig poster. I chose the band Arcade Fire and designed a poster with three variations based on an abstraction of an aerial view of a Haitian village, as the country is dear to the band and an inspiration for their latest record.
