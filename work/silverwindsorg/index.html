September 2014

Silverwinds is a Hutterite colony in Manitoba. They have on-site education as well as providing some educational materials to other colonies in Canada and the US. I developed a website, based on Wordpress, that allowed them to provide lessons and course materials to their students across devices. I worked closely with the principal who provided student feedback which helped me learn how the students were interacting with the course material.

Most of the heavy development is restricted to those with accounts to the website, but you can view the website at silverwinds.org.
